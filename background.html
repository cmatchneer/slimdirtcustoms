The Legend of Slimdirt

Join me stranger and let me tell you a tale of the legend of Slimdirt
He was a man with a vast collection of figures ranging as far the eye could see. One day he decided to make some minor changes to one of his figures. This was the spark that over time as he made more and more customizations to his vast collection grew into a wild raging fire. He began making figures from scratch for fun and to test his skills. Now his services are available to all who wish to have custom figures made for their joy.
